OpenClaw HexonBot

An open-source hexapod robotics platform focused on clean mechanics, modular electronics, and hackable control software.

GitHub Documentation Hardware Community

Why HexonBot?

HexonBot is designed for experimentation. Every part is open, documented, and meant to be modified — from the claw geometry to the gait algorithms.

What’s inside

• Parametric mechanical design
• OpenClaw gripper system
• Modular servo & controller layout
• Firmware and simulation support

Status

Actively developed. Expect breaking changes, frequent improvements, and thoughtful design decisions.
